## 🎯 What is CodeQuity?

**CodeQuity** is **India's largest and most inclusive tech community**, founded with a revolutionary vision to democratize tech education and create equal opportunities for developers across all backgrounds.

### 🌟 **Our Core Mission**
> **"Making world-class tech education accessible to every Indian developer, regardless of their background, location, or economic status."**

### 📊 **Community Impact by Numbers**
- **50,000+** Active Community Members
- **500+** Tech Events & Workshops Hosted
- **100+** Open Source Projects Initiated
- **25** Indian Cities with Active Chapters
- **10,000+** Developers Placed in Top Tech Companies
- **₹50+ Crores** Worth Scholarships Distributed
