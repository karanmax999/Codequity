## [Metadata Guide]
- **Title**: Action-oriented, under 60 characters. (e.g., "The Future of Web3 Startup Foundries")
- **Slug**: Hyphenated, all lowercase. (e.g., "future-of-web3-foundries")
- **Excerpt**: 150-160 characters for SEO snippet.
- **Image URL**: High-quality (1920x1080) .webp or .jpg links.
